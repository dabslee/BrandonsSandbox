Evidence Card E

Evidence

As shown on the evidence card, most earthquakes seem to be hovering around the Middle America Trench.

Reasoning

Looking back at the Plate Motion SIM, we can test whether or not earthquakes seem to happen in trenches, by making the boundary convergent we can test whether or not earthquakes like to hover around trenches. This is impossible for divergent boundaries since trenches cannot form for them.
